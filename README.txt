To see the handin, start up the TomCat-server and run http://localhost:8080/Handin4/products.html

Login or create an account, by typing in the textfield and press a button. 

You can now add items to your cart and see the totalcost in the upper-right corner. 

Buy the items by pressing the  buy-button. 

After you have bought your items, you should press the refresh-button to update the list of items in the table.
